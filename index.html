Physiology

Desensitization is a built-in means for the human body to become accustomed to different stressors. Just as one grows a muscle by lifting a slightly heavier object each training session, so can one accustom their body to handling allergens. Two types of treatments for allergies are oral immunotherapy - a treatment where the patient consumes a small amount of the allergen - and direct injection of the allergen.
